# ⚖️ NyayaShield | AI-Powered Legal Assistant

[cite_start]NyayaShield is a professional legal and consumer complaint drafting assistant designed to bridge the gap between Indian legal complexity and common citizens. [cite: 15, 16]

## 🚀 Live Demo
**Check it out here:** [https://project-rakshak-tr98.onrender.com](https://project-rakshak-tr98.onrender.com)

## 🛠️ The Problem
[cite_start]In India, high legal complexity and a lack of awareness regarding rights (BNS/BSA) often prevent citizens from seeking justice. [cite: 17]

## ✨ The Solution
[cite_start]NyayaShield uses a custom "Hinglish" AI engine to convert informal, conversational user inputs into formal, sophisticated, and court-ready legal drafts. [cite: 18]

### Key Features:
* [cite_start]**Hinglish Support:** Understands natural Indian language patterns. [cite: 18]
* **Formal Drafting:** Generates authoritative and serious legal complaints.
* [cite_start]**Viral Growth Integration:** Built-in WhatsApp sharing mechanism for organic user acquisition. [cite: 20]
